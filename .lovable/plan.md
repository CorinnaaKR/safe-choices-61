

## Child Protection Safeguarding Simulation

An immersive, story-driven training tool for professionals to practice making safeguarding decisions in realistic scenarios.

### The Experience

**Opening Scene**
Users are introduced to their role (e.g., teacher, social worker, or school staff member) and the child at the center of the scenario. The story unfolds with descriptive narrative text that draws them into the situation.

**The Scenario**
A single, detailed child protection case unfolds across multiple scenes. Users will encounter a child (let's call them "Jamie") whose situation becomes increasingly concerning. The story progresses through natural touchpoints - classroom observations, playground interactions, parent meetings, and conversations with colleagues.

---

### Core Features

**Evidence Collection System**
- **Evidence journal** that stores items as users discover them
- Evidence types include:
  - Text messages and social media screenshots
  - Behavioral observations (noted during story scenes)
  - Visual evidence (photos of injuries, concerning drawings)
- Users can review collected evidence before making key decisions

**Branching Decision Points**
At critical moments, users must choose how to respond:
- Who to report concerns to
- When to escalate
- How to approach conversations with the child or parents
- What information to share and with whom

**Immediate Feedback**
After each decision:
- See the consequence unfold in the story
- Receive feedback explaining why the choice was helpful or problematic
- Learn what best practice would recommend

**Realistic Outcomes**
The story leads to different endings based on cumulative decisions:
- Best outcome: Child protected, appropriate intervention
- Mixed outcomes: Delayed help, partial protection
- Poor outcomes: Missed opportunities, escalated risk
- Each ending explains the real-world implications

---

### Progress & Scoring

**During the scenario:**
- Progress indicator showing story advancement
- Evidence counter tracking items collected
- Saves automatically to browser (can resume later)

**At completion:**
- Overall score based on decision quality
- Breakdown of correct vs. problematic choices
- Summary of evidence usage
- Certificate-style completion screen (printable)
- Option to replay and try different choices

---

### Design Approach

**Story-driven & immersive:**
- Narrative prose that reads like a case study
- Character names and realistic dialogue
- Emotional weight without being gratuitously distressing
- Professional but engaging visual design
- Clear typography for easy reading

**Navigation:**
- Clean, focused interface that keeps users in the story
- Evidence panel accessible but not distracting
- Clear indication of decision points vs. story progression
